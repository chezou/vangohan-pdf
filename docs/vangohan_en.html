Chop Suey

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil
  • 200ml water
  • 30ml (2 table spoon) water
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry pork on medium heat with cooking oil, and cook vegetables and mushroom first on high heat for a few mins, then add squid
  2. When ingredients are mostly cooked, add Seasoning② and 200ml water then cook on medium heat for 5 mins
  3. When carrots are soft, adjust the taste with salt and pepper
  4. Mix 30ml water and potato starch then add it into the pan and stir lightly

Miso Stewed Udon

Things you need to prepare

  • 400ml water

Instructions

  1. Boil 400ml water in a pot and dissolve miso
  2. Put shiitake mushroom and fried tofu keep cooking it for 2-3 mins
  3. Add noodles and cook it for a few mins
  4. Put noodles and soup in a bowl, add green onion
  5. Custom your udon with other vegetables and eggs as you like!

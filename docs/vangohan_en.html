Beef Stew

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil
  • 250ml water

Instructions

  1. Coat beef with flour, then stir-fry in a pot with cooking oil
  2. When beef is browned, add and cook vegetables
  3. Add Seasoning③ for a few mins. Add 250ml water and stew it
  4. When beef gets soft, add Seasoning④ and cook on low heat for a while until the sauce gets thick
  5. Mix butter at the end

Salmon and Takana Rice Flavouring

Things you need to prepare

Instructions

  1. Mix the Package with cooked rice (2 people: 2cups)

Stir-Fried Ginger Pork

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry onion on a pan with cooking oil on medium heat until it’s soft
  2. Coat pork with flour and cook until lightly cooked
  3. Add Seasoning③, then cook on low-medium heat until the sauce is thickened. Arrange it with vegetables on a plate.

Sweet & Sour Chilli Fried Chicken

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoon water (30ml)

Instructions

  1. Season Chicken with Seasoning① then coat it with potato starch
  2. Heat a pan with oil and cook Chicken until the colour changes
  3. Add Vegetables, Seasoning③, and 2 tablespoon water and stair-fry
  4. Please make sure chicken is cooked through

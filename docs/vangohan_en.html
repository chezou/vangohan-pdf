Pork Chop

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Salt & pepper and coat pork with flour
  2. Cook with cooking oil on medium heat.
  3. After both side is browned, turn the heat on low and make sure the pork is cooked well
  4. Remove pork and clean the pan lightly
  5. Heat Seasoning⑤ in the pan then add the cooked pork and let it get coated in the sauce.
  6. Arrange with lettuce and lemon on your plate

Soba Noodle with Curry Soup

Things you need to prepare

  • お湯

Instructions

  1. Mix Seasoning① and 150-200ml hot water and boil (adjust the taste by adding less or more water as you like)
  2. Boil soba noodles for 4 mins in a different pot
  3. Drain water from noodles and arrange noodles and soup in a bowl. Add green onions on top

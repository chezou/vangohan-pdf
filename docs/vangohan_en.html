Beef Bowl

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooked rice
  • Egg yolk / Quail egg （Optional）

Instructions

  1. Put Seasoning①, beef, Vegetables, in a pan and cook over medium heat
  2. When boiled, skim the foam from the surface and simmer for about 5 mins with a lid on
  3. Serve it with the broth on rice in a bowl.
  4. Add ginger④ and an egg yolk / quail egg as you like

Mushroom Mixed Rice Base

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooked rice (1-1.5 rice cups)

Instructions

  1. Mix the Package with cooked rice (1 to 1.5 rice cups)
  2. Adjust the taste with soy sauce or salt
  3. Please adjust the taste by mixing more/less rice

Sweet & Sour Pork

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil
  • 2 table spoon water

Instructions

  1. Marinate pork with Seasoning① for 10 mins, then coat it with potato starch①
  2. Stir-fry pork with cooking oil on medium heat. Add carrots and other vegetables. (Microwave carrots for a few mins if you like soft carrots!)
  3. When vegetables are cooked, keep them on side of the pan and heat Seasoning④ and 2 table spoon water on the other side of the pan. (Mix the Seasoning ④ well before use)
  4. Mix well and done!

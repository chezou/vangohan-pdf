Twice-cooked Pork

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat Seasoning① on a pan on low heat until it smells well (add more cooking oil if needed)
  2. Cook pork on medium heat until it’s cooked, then add cabbage and bell pepper on high heat
  3. Mix Seasoning③ when vegetables are cooked

Tempura Fried Chicken

Things you need to prepare

  • Cooking oil
  • 1 Beaten egg
  • 70ml water

Instructions

  1. Mix a beaten egg, 70ml water, and Potato starch① in a bowl
  2. Coat chicken entirely with 1
  3. Pre-heat about 3cm of cooking oil in a pan or a pot at 340F (dip the handle of a wooden spoon or a chopstick into the oil, and the oil starts steadily bubbling, then the oil is hot enough)
  4. Fry chicken about 7 mins on medium heat. Drain excess oil when cooked
  5. Arrange on a plate with vegetables
  6. Please make sure chicken is well cooked

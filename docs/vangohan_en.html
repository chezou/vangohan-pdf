Marmalade Beef Stew

Things you need to prepare

  • Salt & pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 100ml water

Instructions

  1. Season Beef with salt & pepper
  2. Heat a pot with olive oil and stir-fry Beef on medium to high heat to brown them
  3. Add Vegetables, Seasoning③ and 100ml water in a pot and cook it on hight heat to bring to boil with a lid on
  4. When it’s get boiled, turn the heat down to low and keep simmering it for 15-20mins

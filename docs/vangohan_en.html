Hainanese Chicken Rice

Things you need to prepare

  • Rice (2 rice cups are recommended)

Instructions

  1. Wash rice, and add water for the amount of rice in your rice cooker, then Mix Seasoning①
  2. Put chicken and green onion on top, and cook rice
  3. When cooked, remove green onion and chicken. Cut chicken in pieces.
  4. Arrange rice on a plate, put chicken, and pour Green onion and Seasoning④ on top with vegetables
